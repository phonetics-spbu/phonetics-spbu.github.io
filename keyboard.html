Keyboard Override Information:

Digits (0-9): Replaced with superscript equivalents (⁰-⁹)

Punctuation: Replaced with special alternatives:

  • ! → ¡ (inverted exclamation)
  • ? → ¿ (inverted question)
  • . → · (middle dot)
  • , → ‚ (low comma)
  • ; → ; (Greek question mark)
  • : → ː (triangular colon)
  • & → ⅋ (turned ampersand)

All other keys: Behave normally
